## Fan Art

Personal, non-commercial fan art of Mina and Miere is welcome.

Fan art may be created and shared online, provided that it is
not presented as official WebMiere or KawaiiEngine material.

Credit to WebMiere or KawaiiEngine (Sashimiso) is appreciated.

## Permission Is Required For

Prior permission is required for:

- Commercial use
- Merchandise
- Paid distribution of derivative artwork
- Redistribution or rehosting of official artwork or source assets
- Tracing or near-identical reproduction of official artwork
- Use of Mina or Miere as another product's, service's, group's,
  or project's official character or mascot
- Use that falsely suggests endorsement by KawaiiEngine
- Use of official artwork for AI model training or datasets
- Sale or distribution of generated or derivative artwork as
  official WebMiere material

## Prohibited Uses

Mina, Miere, and official WebMiere artwork may not be used for:

- Illegal activity
- Defamation, harassment, or hateful content
- Misleading impersonation of KawaiiEngine or WebMiere
- Political or religious campaigning
- Material that materially damages the identity or reputation
  of the characters or project
